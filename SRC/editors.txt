TUI text editors are not considered because they will support UNICODE poorly or not at all.
Text editors that require personal information for download are not considered
The more an editor costs, the less it scores. Money should purchase excessive features.
Reviews are based on the menu contents, the Find & replace dialog, and the print dialog.

Excellent (Substantial functionality above Notepad++ & NPPTextFX):
  Notepad++
  HTML-Kit
  PSPAD

Good (Some functionality above Notepad++ & NPPTextFX):
  Boxer

Poor (Little or no functionality above Notepad++ & NPPTextFX):
  Textpad
  UltraEdit
  Crimson Editor
